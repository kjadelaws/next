About NEXT

When I returned home after incarceration, I quickly realized that reentry wasn't just about finding a job or getting an ID—it was about trying to figure out what to do next while feeling overwhelmed by everything at once.

I created NEXT because I wanted to build the resource I wish I had when I came home. My hope is that even one clear next step can help someone feel a little less overwhelmed and a little more confident about moving forward.

This is just the beginning. My goal is to use technology to make reentry more accessible, less overwhelming, and more successful through practical guidance and real-world experience.

Every journey begins with the next step forward.
